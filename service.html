About Us

Assure Payroll holds exclusive accreditation.

This means our clients are assured that our business cares about best practice and understands the importance of having a fit-for-purpose and compliant payroll function.

Whether you’re a micro business or a huge company employing hundreds we have the skills and experience to ensure that your payroll is administered efficiently and effectively every month. We have a wide range of clients across Cornwall and the South West, from a variety of employment sectors. Our client’s staff numbers range from 1 – 250 employees.

Wherever you’re based, we can help; we cover all of Cornwall and the South West including Truro, Falmouth, Penryn, Penzance, St Ives, Helston, Hayle, Camborne, Redruth, Pool, St Austell, Newquay, Bodmin, Wadebridge, Padstow, Bude, Launceston, Liskeard, Looe, Saltash and Plymouth.
