  • What Does a Payroll Bureau Do?

    A payroll bureau involves a collaborative effort between in-house payroll staff and an outsourced payroll company. This collaboration allows for a balance: your in-house team handles processes while external experts provide crucial checks and balances. How Do We Process BACS Payments?

  • What is BACS, and why use it?

    Bacs is a UK-based payment system facilitating direct fund transfers between bank accounts. Managed by Bacs Payment Schemes Limited, consisting of 16 leading UK banks and building societies, Bacs guarantees fast, safe, and secure transactions. Utilizing a Bacs Approved Bureau offers simplicity, speed, efficiency, security, and flexibility in managing payments.

  • What is a Bacs Approved Bureau?

    A Bacs Approved Bureau undergoes a stringent inspection and acceptance process by Bacs. These bureaus are regularly audited to maintain high standards in procedures and security. Choosing a Bacs Approved Bureau like Pay plus ensures safe payment handling, saving time and money and reducing stress for your business.
