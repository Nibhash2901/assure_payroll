Faq’s

Do you have any questions? We have answers!

  • What are managed payroll services in the UK?

    Managed Payroll Services involve engaging a third-party vendor to help you with payroll processing and offer dedicated support when you need it. This service is comprised of assisting you in specific parts of the entire payroll process, maximizing accuracy, efficiency, and punctuality throughout the payroll cycle.

  • Why should I consider payroll outsourcing?

    Outsourcing payroll operations can yield significant benefits by saving time, optimizing resources, and curbing payroll discrepancies. Collaborating with a proficient partner grants access to specialized country-specific payroll expertise and cutting-edge technology, fostering the growth and adaptability of your organization.

  • How can I transfer my payroll data efficiently?

    Transferring data includes various methods, and at Assure Payroll, we accommodate your preferred approach. Together, we create processing timelines for a smooth transition.

  • Will I lose oversight of my payroll?

    While entrusting your payroll to a third party, maintaining control and peace of mind is crucial. Our standardized forms streamline the process, yet we prioritize tailoring solutions to your specific needs. Whether it's standard procedures or custom reports, we collaborate to ensure you stay connected and in control of your payroll operations.

  • How will my payroll bureau interact with HMRC?

    Upon completion of the 'Authorising your agent form' (64-8), your payroll bureau becomes authorized to receive tax notices and handle payroll submissions like FPS and EPS on your behalf. They can also manage HMRC-related queries, ensuring compliance with required reporting standards on each payday.
